Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is the perfect comfort food.

Layered with rich meat sauce and creamy cheese, it's always a favorite.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles and prepare sauce.
  3. Layer sauce, noodles, and cheese.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes.
  5. Let rest before serving.
